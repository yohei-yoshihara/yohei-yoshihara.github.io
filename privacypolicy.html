Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in my Applications (MySimplePlan and Magquash).

I Collect No Personal Information Using My Applications

I do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data recorded in my Applications.

Individual settings relating to my Applications are not personal and are stored only on your device. You might also be asked to provide access to your photo library, but this is only so you can open your photos in my Applications and save images to your photo library. I don’t process that information at all and have no access to it.

In addition, if you take a photo using my Applications, location data is saved in the image, but I have no access to that data and it is not shared with anyone unless you yourself choose to share the image.

Log Data

I want to inform you that whenever you use my Applications, in a case of an error in my Applications I collect data and information on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing my Applications, the time and date of your use of the Applications, and other statistics.

My Applications Comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act

My applications don’t collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
